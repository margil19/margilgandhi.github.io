About

Beyond the resume

I'm a systems thinker who wants to build meaningful things at scale—and bring people along while doing it. I don't just build products; I question whether the work is meaningful, whether it's actually helping people, and whether I'm creating leverage for others to grow faster too.

I'm someone who's always hunting for the best local spots to eat, loves deep conversations about philosophy, life, purpose, and product, and genuinely cares about people. That combination of doing and reflecting is what drives me—I build things, then think deeply about whether they matter.

I grew up in Mumbai, studied in Syracuse, and now call Richardson, TX home. My projects reflect how I think and learn—whether it's Think Again (a reflection tool) or mentoring 2,500+ students. I carry a non-zero-sum mindset: success isn't something to compete for, it's something to expand and share.
